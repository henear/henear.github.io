In the Academia setting, my interest lies on numerical analysis and interpretation of the data.
In spring and summer 2016, I worked on condition number estimation under the supervision of Professor Robert A. van de Geijn.

Key Idea

Denote conditon number of A as cond(A), by definition, cond(A) = ||A||*||A-1||. We know that ||A-1||*||x||>=||A-1x|| = ||b||.
By trying different b to solve different x , use the ratio ||b|| over ||x|| as the approximation of ||A-1||.
In this way, we get the norm without explicitly calculating the inverse of the matrix and thus reduce the complexity.
